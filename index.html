What is Apple Harvest Festival?

The 35th Annual Great Downtown Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival by Presented Tompkins Trust Company takes place September 29 (12pm-6pm), September 30 (10am-6pm), and October 1, 2017 (10am-6pm). The 2017 Apple Harvest Festival celebrates the local farms and vendors of Ithaca. There will be fresh baked goods, produce, crafts, games, rides, performances, and a lot of apples!
